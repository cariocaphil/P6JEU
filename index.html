Board Wars

A Board Game for Two Players

Rules: Press arrow keys for moving your player. Every player has three moves per turn.

Try to pick up a good weapon asap. If you move to a field where your opponent is placed, a combat between the two of you will start.

During combat every player has only one move per turn. You can decide to defend or attack. During an attack the damage depends on the force of the weapon used for the attack.

Each player has 100 life points. The player who first gets the opponent to loose all life points wins.
